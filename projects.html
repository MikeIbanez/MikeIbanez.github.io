Saenger III - Conceptual SSBJ

Stability and Control analysis tools and methodologies of a Hypersonic Transportation Vehicle. This work was produced for UTA's Fall 2019 Aerospace Engineering Senior Design Project.

YardEye - CV Speed Detector

Summer Intern Project developed for BNSF Railway as an alternative to radars on sorting yards. This program was able to take the video feed from low-end off the shelf cameras and produce speed readings in the +/- 0.5 mph range.

Project Alan - Arduino CNC Plotter

Arduino based IoT CNC Plotter able to take pictures in PNG or Jpeg formats through a webportal and produce the G-Code required by the motor drivers to draw the image. This platform can be adapted to utilizie different attachments.
